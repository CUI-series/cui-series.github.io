Human-Robot Conversational Interaction

Join us in Stockholm in March 2023

Attending the Workshop

Schedule:

  • 09:00 - 09:05: Opening remarks
  • 09:05 - 09:45: Keynote by Dr. Cristina Zaga
  • 09:45 - 10:25: Author presentations from the 3-4 papers most relevant to the disussion
  • 10:25 - 10:40: Coffee break
  • 10:40 - 11:25: Group Discussion: Identifying shared goals, challenges and potential future projects for HR-C-I
  • 11:25 - 12:00: Report back & collect ideas for participants to collaborate on HRI Provocations papers
  • 12:00 - 12:45: Provocations Kickstart. Groups around each prospective paper sketch the contents and design prototypes or experiments
  • 12:45 - 13:00: Wrap up with findings from the workshop

Keynote Speaker:
Dr. ir. Cristina Zaga is Assistant Professor at Human-Centred Design Group (Design and Production Management department) and a researcher at The DesignLab at the University of Twente. Her contribution is in design methods for embodied AI and interactive agency. Her interests lay in the interaction design and related methodologies for technological products and systems that aim at social good, especially physically embodied AI-driven systems
